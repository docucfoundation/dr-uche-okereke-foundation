Why This Scholarship Exists

Opportunity Should Not Depend on Circumstance

Dr. Uche Okereke created this scholarship from a deeply held belief: talent exists in every corner of Ovim, but opportunity does not. This initiative bridges that gap — giving promising students from disadvantaged backgrounds the support they need to succeed.

Beyond covering tuition, the program builds leadership skills and connects scholars with mentors — preparing them to become change makers in their own communities.

Who Can Apply

Is This Scholarship For You?

The scholarship is open to Ovim youthss who meet the following criteria. Read carefully before starting your application.

🎂

Age 17–25

Applicants must be between 17 and 25 years of age at the time of application.

🏫

Nigerian Institution

You have gained admission or are in your first year at a recognized Nigerian tertiary institution.

💸

Financial Hardship

You are experiencing significant financial hardship — such as loss of a parent, displacement, or family crisis.

🏘️

Ovim Youths

You are from Ovim and are committed to volunteering and giving back to the Ovim community.

📋

Academic Potential

You can demonstrate academic motivation and a personal story that shows drive and resilience.

💌

Ready to Apply

You have a personal statement, admission/enrollment proof, academic records, and two references ready.

Application Process

From Application to Award

1

Complete the Form

Fill out the online application and upload all required supporting documents by July 31, 2026.

2

Committee Review

A selection committee evaluates each application based on hardship, personal story, academic potential, and commitment to Ovim.

3

Interview (If Shortlisted)

Shortlisted candidates may be invited for an in-person or virtual interview with the selection committee.

4

Award by August 15

Successful applicants are notified by August 15, 2026 and welcomed into the scholarship program.
