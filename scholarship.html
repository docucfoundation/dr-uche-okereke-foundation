Need-Based Award

The Scholarship Program

Full details on the award, eligibility, expectations, and how the selection process works.

What the Scholarship Covers

The Award Package

Each year, selected scholars receive up to ₦250,000 to help with the following, for up to four years.

🏫
Tuition
Full or partial tuition fees covered
📋
School Fees
All compulsory institutional fees
📚
Book Support
Academic materials and textbooks
💵
Cash Balance
Any remaining funds disbursed to the awardee

Up to ₦250,000 per year · Up to 4 years duration

Funds are disbursed to cover tuition and compulsory fees first. Any remaining balance is paid directly to the scholar.

Eligibility Criteria

Who Can Apply

The scholarship is open to Ovim youths who meet all of the following criteria. If you're unsure, contact us before applying.

🎂 Age

  • ✓ Aged between 17 and 25 years at the time of application

🏫 Enrollment

  • ✓ Have gained admission to, or are currently in your first year of study at a recognized Nigerian tertiary institution

💸 Financial Hardship

  • ✓ Experiencing significant financial hardship or personal challenges — for example: loss of a parent, displacement, family crisis, or similar circumstances

🏘️ Community Commitment

  • ✓ From Ovim and committed to volunteering and giving back to the Ovim community

📄 Application Requirements

To complete your application, you must fill out the online form and provide the following documents:

✓ Personal Statement — 600-800 words
✓ Admission letter or proof of first-year enrollment / academic result
✓ Available academic records — O'Level (WAEC, NECO, GCE or equivalent) results, Official Transcript
✓ Two recommendations — teachers, mentors, religious leaders, or community leaders
✓ Proof of hardship — e.g. parent's death certificate, displacement record, or similar documentation
✓ A passport photograph in decent dressing

Scholarship Conditions

Expectations of Awardees

Recipients of the scholarship are expected to uphold the following responsibilities throughout the duration of the award. These conditions ensure that scholars remain focused, accountable, and committed to the values of the program.

📊
Maintain Academic Excellence
Submit verifiable annual academic progress reports to the scholarship committee.
🎓
Complete Studies on Time
Complete your studies within the expected time frame for your programme.
🏘️
Community Outreach
Participate in community outreach activities in Ovim as directed by the program.
🚫
Approved Groups Only
Not join or participate in any non-approved school groups, or any external functions banned by the government, during the scholarship period.
📋
Authentic Documents Only
Not submit any falsified or fraudulent documents to influence selection or continued receipt of the award. Doing so will result in immediate disqualification.

Selection Criteria

How We Choose Scholars

Applications are reviewed by a selection committee based on four core criteria.

1

Degree of Financial Hardship

The severity and impact of your financial circumstances on your ability to access education.

2

Personal Story

The strength and authenticity of your personal statement — your journey, resilience, and character.

3

Academic Potential

Your academic record and the motivation you demonstrate to succeed in your chosen field.

4

Willingness to Serve

Your commitment to give back to the Ovim community as part of the scholarship program.

Note: Shortlisted applicants may be invited for an in-person or virtual interview.
Successful applicants will be notified by September 15, 2026.
