Eligibility Criteria

Scholarship Requirements

All applicants must meet the following requirements. If you have questions about your eligibility, please contact us before applying.

📖 Academic Standing

  • ✓ Minimum GPA of 3.5 / 5.0, or equivalent in your grading system
  • ✓ First-year students without prior results may still apply and will be assessed on high school performance
  • ✓ Must be enrolled in or admitted to an accredited university or college

🎓 Year of Study

  • ✓ Applicants must be first or second-year undergraduate students
  • ✓ Recent high school leavers with confirmed university admission are also eligible

🔬 Field of Study

  • ✓ STEM disciplines: Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics
  • ✓ Related fields may be considered on a case-by-case basis — contact us if you're unsure

💸 Financial Hardship

  • ✓ Applicant must demonstrate significant financial need
  • ✓ Priority given to students from single-parent households, families with disability, or households with an unemployed caregiver
  • ✓ Supporting financial documentation will be required as part of the application

📄 Documents You'll Need

📋 Academic transcript or results
🪪 Government-issued ID
🏫 Proof of enrollment or admission letter
💌 Personal statement (500–800 words)
💸 Proof of financial hardship
✉️ One letter of recommendation

Selection Process

How We Choose Scholars

1

Application Review

All complete applications are reviewed by our selection committee against the eligibility criteria.

2

Shortlisting

Top candidates are shortlisted based on academic performance, financial need, and demonstrated potential.

3

Interview

Shortlisted applicants are invited to a brief virtual or in-person interview with foundation representatives.

4

Award

Final scholars are selected, notified, and welcomed into the foundation's community and programs.
