Common Questions

When do applications open and close?

Applications open in the fall semester and close December 31st. Check the application page for the current cycle's exact dates.

Can I apply if I'm not in a STEM field?

Our primary focus is STEM. Non-STEM applicants may be considered on a case-by-case basis — please contact us before applying.

Is the scholarship renewable each year?

Scholars who maintain their GPA and remain in good standing may be considered for renewal. Renewal is not automatic and must be applied for each year.

How will I know if my application was received?

You'll receive an automatic email confirmation immediately after submitting your application. Our team will follow up within 4–6 weeks.

How can I volunteer as a mentor?

We love that you want to give back! Please fill out the contact form and select "Volunteer as Mentor" — our programs team will be in touch.
