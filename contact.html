Frequently Asked Questions

When is the application deadline?

The 2026 application deadline is August 20, 2026. Award announcements will be made by September 15, 2026. Late applications will not be considered.

Do I have to be from Ovim to apply?

Yes. This scholarship is specifically for Ovim youth. You must have a genuine connection to the Ovim community and be committed to giving back to it.

Can I apply if I haven't started university yet?

Yes. The scholarship is open to applicants who have gained admission to a Nigerian tertiary institution, even if they haven't started yet. You will need to provide your admission letter.

What does "significant financial hardship" mean?

Examples include loss of a parent, displacement, family crisis, or extreme poverty. You must provide documentary proof of your hardship (e.g. a death certificate, displacement record, etc.).

Is the scholarship renewable each year?

Yes, the scholarship is designed to last up to four years. However, continued funding each year is contingent on the scholar maintaining academic excellence and submitting verifiable annual progress reports.

How can I support or donate to the scholarship?

You can donate securely online via our Donate page using Stripe or Paystack. For grants or partnership inquiries, contact us at contact@docucfoundation.org or fill out the contact form and select "Donation / Partnership Inquiry."
