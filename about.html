Founder's Vision

Why Dr. Uche Okereke Created This Scholarship

"I believe that talent exists in every corner of Ovim, but opportunity does not. The Dr. Uche Okereke Youth Educational Scholarship was created to bridge that gap — giving promising students from disadvantaged backgrounds the support they need to succeed academically, personally, and professionally."

The goal is clear: to build a pipeline of future-ready, socially conscious leaders who will give back to the Ovim community — creating a cycle of impact that extends far beyond the individual scholarship recipient.

This scholarship is Dr. Okereke's personal commitment to educational equality, and a tribute to every young person in Ovim who has the drive to succeed but lacks the financial means to do so.

More Than Financial Aid

A Transformational Program

This scholarship offers more than money. It is a comprehensive program designed to develop the whole person.

💰

Financial Support

Up to ₦200,000 per year for up to four years, covering tuition, compulsory school fees, and book support.

🧭

Mentorship

Periodic mentorship sessions that connect scholars with experienced professionals and community leaders.

🏆

Leadership Development

Structured opportunities to build leadership skills that prepare scholars to be change makers in Ovim.

🤲

Community Service

Scholars participate in community outreach in Ovim, giving back as part of their scholarship commitment.

📊

Accountability

Annual academic progress reviews ensure scholars stay on track and continue to meet program standards.

🔗

Lasting Network

Scholars join a community of like-minded peers and mentors committed to the development of Ovim.
